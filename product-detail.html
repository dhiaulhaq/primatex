Product Description

PrimaTex Antimicrobial Bed Sheets are specifically designed for healthcare environments where cleanliness and infection control are paramount. Our sheets are made from a premium cotton-polyester blend that provides both comfort and durability, while our proprietary antimicrobial treatment helps prevent the growth of bacteria, fungi, and other microorganisms.

These bed sheets are ideal for hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, and other healthcare facilities. They are designed to withstand frequent washing at high temperatures without losing their antimicrobial properties or color fastness.

Benefits
  • Reduces the risk of healthcare-associated infections
  • Provides a comfortable and hygienic environment for patients
  • Durable construction saves on replacement costs
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Available in various sizes to fit all standard hospital beds
